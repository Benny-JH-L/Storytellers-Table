
### Keybinds ###

## Camera ##
WASD/Arrow keys:			move horizontally
Q/E:						to move the camera down/up (vertical movement)

# Mouse Dragging #
middle mouse + shift key:									horizontal movment
middle mouse + ctrl/command key:							vertical movement
Middle mouse without shift, or ctr/command keys pressed:	camera rotation


## Campaign Modes ##
c: cycle between modes

# Map Edit #
